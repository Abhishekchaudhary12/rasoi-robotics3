Our Journey

Discover how Rasoi Robotics began and where we are headed.

The Beginning

At Rasoi Robotics, we aim to innovate with smart kitchen equipment that significantly reduces the reliance on trained chefs, potentially decreasing their involvement by at least 70%. By leveraging cutting-edge technology, we strive to make commercial kitchen operations seamless and cost-effective.Rasoi Robotics started with a vision to revolutionize the way cooking is done in households and commercial kitchens. Our mission is to make cooking easier, smarter, and more efficient using robotics technology.

Our Growth

Culinario:

A versatile Smart Kitchen Assistant designed to automate various Gravy cooking tasks, ensuring consistency and efficiency in the kitchen.


DosaGenie:

A specialized machine that automates the process of making Dosas, offering precision and speed, and eliminating the need for skilled labor.Over the years, we have expanded our operations, perfected our product, and grown into a team of experts passionate about innovation in culinary automation. Today, we continue to push boundaries with our advanced cooking robots designed to handle various kitchen tasks effortlessly.

The Future

As we look to the future, we are excited about the endless possibilities that Rasoi Robotics can bring to kitchens around the world. We are constantly evolving and innovating to bring smarter solutions to the culinary world.
